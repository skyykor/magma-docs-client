# Notifications

The **Notifications** section stores all notifications from pop-ups that have been shown to you.

![Notifications List](assets/images/notifications/notifications-list.png)

## What You Can See

For each notification the following information is displayed:

- **Name** — notification title
- **Views** — how many times it was viewed
- **Last shown** — the date it was last displayed

## How to View a Notification

1. Click the **"View"** button next to the notification
2. A window with the full notification details will open

![Notifications View](assets/images/notifications/notifications-view.png)
